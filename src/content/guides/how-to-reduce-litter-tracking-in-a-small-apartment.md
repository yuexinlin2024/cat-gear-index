## Treat Tracking As A Room Problem

In a small apartment, litter tracking travels farther because there is less separation between the litter zone and the rest of daily life. That is why the fix usually needs to involve the space around the box, not only the litter inside it.

## Start With Exit Behavior

Watch how the cat actually leaves the box. Some cats step out carefully. Others jump, dig, or launch sideways. That behavior tells you whether the fix should focus on mat placement, box height, exit clearance, or litter texture.

## Reduce Friction In The Cleanup

If the cleanup process is annoying, tracking gets worse because it sits longer on the floor. Keep a small broom, handheld vacuum, or easy pickup tool close to the box so that reset time stays short.

## Avoid Overcrowding The Corner

Trying to solve tracking by piling on too many mats, bins, and blockers can make the area harder to clean. In a small apartment, a simpler cleaner system usually wins over a more complicated setup.
