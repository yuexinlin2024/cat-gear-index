## Buy For The Real Task

The towel does not need to look special. It needs to absorb water quickly, feel soft enough for repeated use, and be easy to grab before the cat starts trying to leave the room.

## Size Helps

A towel that is too small forces more handling when the cat is already uncomfortable. Slightly larger towels are usually easier because they wrap faster and reduce the need for repeated repositioning.

## Keep More Than One

For home bathing, two practical towels often work better than one expensive towel. One can handle the first heavy moisture and the second can finish the job more comfortably.

## Avoid Overthinking Branding

Many owners do not need a towel marketed specifically for pets. The more important questions are whether it dries well, washes cleanly, and stays soft enough to use again without fuss.
