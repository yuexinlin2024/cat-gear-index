## Prioritize Footprint

Small apartments need products that solve the job without spreading mess into the whole room. Litter tracking, food scatter, water splashes, and unstable cat trees become more obvious in tight spaces.

## Use Vertical Space

A compact cat tree, wall shelves, or window perch can add territory without taking much floor space. For tall products, check stability and anchoring options.

## Keep Maintenance Easy

If a product is hard to clean, it becomes harder to tolerate in a small space. Prioritize removable parts, washable mats, accessible pumps, and litter boxes that can be scooped without awkward handling.
