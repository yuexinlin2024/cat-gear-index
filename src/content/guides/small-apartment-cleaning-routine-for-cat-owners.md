## Build Around The Highest-Friction Areas

In a small apartment, a few zones create most of the visible mess: the litter corner, the sleeping fabric, and the floor around the cat's main walking path. A good routine focuses there first instead of trying to clean everything equally.

## Use One Short Weekly Reset

Pick a repeatable block of time and run the same order each week: scoop and reset the litter zone, shake or wash fabric layers, brush loose hair from key surfaces, and return grooming tools to their place. Routine matters more than perfection.

## Keep Supplies In One Place

If the cleaning tools are split across several rooms, the routine feels heavier than it is. A compact home works better when the main cleanup tools stay near the areas that need them most.

## Let The Routine Stay Small

This should not feel like a major house project. The best version is short enough that you actually repeat it, because repeated maintenance keeps the apartment easier to live in than occasional big cleanup sessions.
