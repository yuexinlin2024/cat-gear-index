## Cleaning Rhythm

Most fountain comparisons focus on capacity and appearance, but cleaning effort matters more over time. A good product record should track basin material, pump access, filter availability, and replacement cost.

## What to Clean

Clean the basin, lid, pump housing, intake areas, and any parts that collect hair or mineral buildup. Follow the product manual and avoid harsh fragrances that may discourage drinking.

## What to Compare

When comparing fountains, record filter SKU, filter price, expected replacement cycle, pump warranty, noise when full, and noise when water level drops.
