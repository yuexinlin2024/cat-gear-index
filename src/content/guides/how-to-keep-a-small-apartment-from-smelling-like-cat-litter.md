## Compact Spaces Need Better Rhythm

Small apartments do not forgive delayed cleanup very well. If waste sits too long, if trash stays indoors too long, or if air flow is poor, the whole room starts carrying the litter smell instead of just the litter corner.

## Focus On The Full System

The box, the mat, the scoop, the trash container, and the room air all matter. It helps to think in terms of one connected system instead of trying to fix everything by switching litters repeatedly.

## Keep The Trash Process Tight

One of the fastest ways to improve smell is to shorten the time scooped waste stays indoors. A cleaner trash routine often improves the room faster than changing litter brands.

## Let Air Flow Help

When possible, give the litter area some consistent air movement. You do not need a dramatic setup. Even simple room flow can reduce how long odor lingers in a tight space.
