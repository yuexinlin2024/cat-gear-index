## The Basic Formula

The easiest estimate is:

```text
monthly cost = cats x pounds used per cat per week x 4.345 x cost per pound
```

This gives a planning number. Actual cost depends on litter type, box count, scooping frequency, humidity, and how often the box is fully replaced.

## Why Bag Price Misleads

A cheaper bag can cost more if it is smaller, tracks more, controls odor poorly, or requires more frequent full replacements. Compare cost per pound and how quickly the litter becomes unusable.
